About Me

My name is Criss Azer, and my story is a profound testament to the power of resilience, determination, and the pursuit of dreams. Born in the historic and culturally rich city of Alexandria, Egypt, a place that has witnessed the ebb and flow of civilizations, my early years were a tapestry of experiences deeply embedded in Egyptian culture, history, and traditions. Growing up in such a historic place instilled in me a sense of pride and a deep connection to my roots. The intricate blend of ancient traditions and modern influences in Alexandria shaped my worldview, giving me a unique perspective on life. These early experiences in a city known for its intellectual and cultural heritage were instrumental in forging the person I would become. They provided a foundation of strong cultural identity and a deep appreciation for history and heritage.

Destiny, however, had a different trajectory planned for me. Recognizing the pivotal role of education and the vast opportunities it could unlock, my parents made a life-altering decision to leave behind a comfortable existence in Saudi Arabia. They chose to embark on a journey to Anaheim, California, envisioning a better future for my brothers and me. This was a decision laden with sacrifice and hope, as they uprooted their lives for the promise of educational opportunities for us. Growing up in Anaheim presented a stark contrast to my life in Alexandria, introducing me to a new culture, a different education system, and a completely new way of life. This dramatic change was challenging, but it also opened doors to new possibilities and learning.

The transition to life in Anaheim was not without its challenges. I found myself navigating through unfamiliar cultural landscapes and adapting to a new education system. I attended Magnolia High School, a school more notorious for its low graduation rate than its academic achievements. Despite these odds, I was determined to excel and carve a path for myself. My journey through high school was marked by long nights of studying and unwavering dedication to my academics. When I graduated with honors, it was a moment of triumph over adversity, a testament to the power of determination and hard work. This achievement was not just a personal success; it symbolized the overcoming of societal expectations and barriers.

The elation of my high school graduation soon led to another milestone in my life. I received acceptance letters from multiple prestigious universities, each letter a symbol of the possibilities that lay ahead. This moment was more than just an academic achievement; it was a validation of the countless hours of hard work and the sacrifices made along the way. Filled with pride and joy, I chose to pursue a degree in engineering, a field that promised intellectual challenges and endless curiosity. My choice to study engineering was driven by a desire to understand how the world works, to solve complex problems, and to make a tangible impact on the world around me.

My journey through the world of engineering was both challenging and exhilarating. The field demanded not just academic excellence but also creativity, precision, and a deep understanding of complex concepts. Throughout my university years, I was deeply engaged in my studies, often delving into intricate engineering problems that required innovative solutions. My hard work and dedication were recognized when I achieved the prestigious Dean’s Honor Roll four times. This accomplishment was a significant milestone, reflecting my academic prowess and my ability to overcome challenges. It was a reminder of how far I had come, from the streets of Alexandria to the halls of higher education, and the numerous obstacles I had surmounted along the way.

However, my life was not solely defined by academic achievements. I have always believed in the importance of a well-rounded personality, and my varied interests are a testament to this belief. Robotics, a field that perfectly blends mechanical and software engineering, has always captivated me. I have devoted countless hours to studying and experimenting with robotics, driven by a fascination with how technology can mimic life. Soccer is another passion of mine, offering not just physical exercise but also a way to understand principles of dynamics and motion. Every match is a new opportunity to apply these principles, blending physical prowess with scientific understanding. Cars have also played a significant role in my life, capturing my imagination with their design, power, and the intricate engineering behind them. My interest in cars has enhanced my understanding of mechanical engineering principles, making me a more competent and insightful engineer.

Soccer is another passion of mine. It’s not just a sport for me, but a way to understand the principles of dynamics and motion. Every game is a new lesson in these principles. The thrill of scoring a goal or making a save is not just about the game, but about understanding the science behind it.

Today, I stand proud not just as an engineer, but as a lifelong learner, a soccer enthusiast, a car aficionado, and a robotics geek. My journey from the ancient streets of Alexandria to the vibrant city of Anaheim, and from a struggling student at Magnolia High School to a successful engineer, is a story of resilience, determination, and the pursuit of dreams. This journey has been about more than just achieving academic and professional success; it has been about growing, learning, and evolving. As I look forward to the future, I am filled with excitement for the new opportunities and challenges that await. I am Criss Azer, and this is my story. Reflecting on my journey, I realize that every challenge, every obstacle, and every triumph has shaped me into the person I am today. I am grateful for these experiences and look forward to what the future holds, knowing that the journey, with all its ups and downs, is what truly matters. And what a journey it has been!
